Welcome to Hawabagh College

Mission Statement

To administer, maintain and promote educational institutions under the Methodist Church in India. To impart value-based education, provide sound learning, inculcate spiritual truths, and build good moral character.

"Send Out Thy Light" - Following our motto since 1928 to produce worthy citizens of India.

College Introduction & Growth

Founded in 1928 by Miss E.L. Clinton near the river Narmada. Administered by the Methodist Church in India.

Growth Timeline

  • 1928: Established as Teachers Training College.
  • 1959: Addition of Arts and Science faculties.
  • 1963: M.Ed. introduced.
  • 1966: Science Block and Gymnasium added.
  • 1996: Commerce Faculty introduced.
  • 2006: BBA, BCA and Bio-Tech introduced.
  • 2020: NEP implemented for all UG levels.
