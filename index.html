Welcome to Hawabagh College

About Our Institution

Hawabagh College, founded in 1928, stands near the bank of river Narmada. Administered by the Methodist Church in India, it is a Pioneer institute for Women's education in Madhya Pradesh.

Academic Programs

We offer various programs under NEP 2020 including B.Com, B.B.A. (Co-Ed), and M.Com.
